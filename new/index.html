Two-day AI workshop

You cannot delegate what you cannot articulate.

Make It So Camp is a selective two-day workshop for people who need to make their AI working method explicit, test it with others, and build enough to show.

Request an invitation See the camps

Generic AI advice starts with delegation.

But most meaningful work is not ready to delegate. It lives in judgment, taste, timing, tacit routines, and half-spoken criteria that teams use without naming.

Make It So Camp begins before the prompt. Day 1 is for making your working method visible: how you frame a problem, decide what matters, and know when something is good enough to use.

Day 2 turns that articulation into shared practice. You build with others, compare methods across domains, and demo what your way of working makes possible.

The point is not to become faster at generic tasks. It is to make your own work legible enough that AI can become a collaborator rather than a shortcut.

Method

Three moves, repeated for real work.
